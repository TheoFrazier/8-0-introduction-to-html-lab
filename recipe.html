The Perfect PB&J

Description

The peanute butter and jelly sandwhich is my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • A butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both pieces of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side.
  3. One the righ side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place to the two halves together so that jelly is on top
  5. With the sharp knife cut the sandwhich in half.
  6. Enjoy!
